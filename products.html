Signum Products

Anywhere and Everywhere

Edge

Signum Edge is a line of internet connectable devices which provide real-time data acquisition. Signum Edge can be used for remote monitoring of resources, safety monitoring in hazardous environments, asset tracking, process control, and many other applications.
Signum Edge devices are designed to work with industry standard sensor types.

Connect

Signum Connect allow you to select the right connection for your application. Near the office? WiFi connectivity should do the trick. Out in the field? Cellular LTE is the right move. Way out there? Satellite data solves that problem too.
Using the latest CAT-M1 and NB-IOT modems, our devices use less power and are economical with your data.
For locations that don’t have access to a cellular network, our Signum Edge device can be equipped with satellite modems ensuring you are always connected.

Cloud

Signum Cloud is your interface for visualizing and understanding the data acquired by your Signum Edge devices. You can customize your views to see the current status as well as historical data for each device.
