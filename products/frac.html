Frac Sand Storage Bins

The Amber Waves frac sand bin was born in Bakken. In fact, anytime you see a row of white bins lined up along a rail siding in oil country, odds are pretty good that you’ll see the Amber Waves logo on them.

Strength, durability and dependability are engineered into every one of our frac sand bins, and they’re built strong enough for even the heaviest ceramic proppants. If you needed bins for your transload in North Dakota’s Bakken last year but this year operations are shifting to the Eagle Ford play in Texas, our 1330 model is easily transportable.
