Overhead Cake Bins

When it comes to overhead cake bins, we designed and built the bin we wanted for our own ranches. We wanted to be able to operate the slide gate with gloves on and load the cake feeder without getting a face full of dust.

In addition, we wanted to be able to see at a glance how much feed we had remaining in the bin. When the bottom view glass shows that it’s empty with our model 1415C, you can see that you have room to take a semi-load and still have feed left for a couple of days.
