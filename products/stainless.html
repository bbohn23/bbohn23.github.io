Stainless Steel Hopper Bins

If you’re looking for the ultimate storage bin for bulk dry fertilizer or other highly corrosive materials and want something that will last, the stainless steel hopper bin will be the perfect fit for you.

The stainless steel smooth wall hopper bin is one of our newest products at Amber Waves. By using 304 stainless steel for the bin body and carbon steel for the support structure, we’ve developed the ultimate value in this product. These bins are available in all of the same sizes as our standard line of grain bins.
